      <Step title="Create Project" icon="plus">

      </Step>
      <Step title="Publish" icon="send">
        Publish your docs and share them with your team or the public.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

    Access your documentation programmatically through our REST API. Retrieve, update, and manage documents with simple HTTP requests.
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="cURL,Python">
      ```bash
curl -X GET "https://api.khaoula-docs.com/v1/documents" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY"
      ```

      ```python

response = requests.get('https://api.khaoula-docs.com/v1/documents',
      ```
